Ferrari 250 TR - Prototipo. 0666 History.

 

Appearing at the Nurburgring in 1957 0666 had an identical body shape to the 290 MM which was similar to that of XCH 555.
Nurburgring May 1957 Gregory / Marolli - 10th.
Sweden 1957 Gendebien / Trintignant DNF Blown engine.
Venezuela 1957 Von Trips / Seidel - 3rd.
Le Mans June 23/24 1957 DNS Piston failure warming up before practice.
Argentina January 26 1958 2nd Hawthorne / Von Tripps.
Taro Florio May 11 1957 DNF Gino Munaron / Seidel.
Le Mans June 21/22 1958 DNF Dan Gurney /Bruce Kessler. DNF Crashed & Burned.
Sebring March 21 1959 DNF Rod Carveth / Gil Geitner.
Nurburgring June 7 1959 DNF Rod Carveth / Gil Geitner
Le Mans June 20 /21 1959 DNF Rod Carveth / Gil Geitner.
Laguna Seca 1960 Phil Hill DNS Did not qualify.
